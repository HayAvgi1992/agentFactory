# Monday CRM

**Category:** Revenue / Sales CRM  
**Best for:** Pipeline visibility, lead tracking, deal management

## Capabilities
- Lead and contact management with custom pipelines
- Email sequences and activity tracking
- Forecasting and revenue reporting
- Integrations with Gmail, Outlook, and marketing tools

## Ideal Customer
- Sales teams of 5–50 reps
- Companies needing pipeline visibility and lead tracking
- B2B SaaS, professional services, fintech
