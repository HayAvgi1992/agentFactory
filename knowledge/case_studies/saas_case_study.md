# SaaS Case Study — CloudStack

**Industry:** SaaS  
**Product:** Work Management  
**Company size:** 120 employees

## Challenge
CloudStack's product and engineering teams used spreadsheets for sprint planning. Cross-team visibility was poor.

## Solution
Rolled out Work Management boards with automations connecting Jira and Slack.

## Results
- 45% reduction in status-update meetings
- On-time delivery improved from 68% to 89%
- Team satisfaction score up 28%

## Relevance
Ideal reference for SaaS companies seeking project management and collaboration solutions.
