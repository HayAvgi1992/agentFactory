# Fintech Case Study — NovaPay

**Industry:** Fintech  
**Product:** Monday CRM  
**Company size:** 250 employees

## Challenge
NovaPay's sales team lacked pipeline visibility. Deals stalled without clear next steps.

## Solution
Deployed Monday CRM with custom pipelines per product line. Integrated email tracking and forecasting.

## Results
- 34% increase in qualified pipeline within 90 days
- 22% faster deal velocity
- SDR-to-AE handoff time reduced by 40%

## Relevance
Strong reference for fintech leads needing pipeline visibility and lead tracking.
