# Qualification Playbook

## ICP Criteria
- Company size: 20–500 employees
- Industries: SaaS, fintech, professional services, e-commerce
- Buying signals: demo request, pricing inquiry, timeline mentioned

## Scoring Guidelines
| Signal | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Demo or pricing request | +20 |
| Urgency (ASAP, this quarter) | +15 |
| Company size 100+ | +10 |
| Known industry fit | +5 |

## Disqualifiers
- Company size under 10 with no budget signal
- Generic spam or job-seeker inquiries
- Competitor research with no buying intent

## Threshold
- Score ≥ 60: Qualified for sales process
- Score ≥ 75: Recommend book_meeting
